01 · Programmes

Research programmes

Current programme

ASCRI: Adversarial Systems & Complexity Research Initiative

A formal programme investigating sufficiently complex dynamic networks of agents where equilibria cannot be reached under formalised friction constraints. These systems function only through a reduction of friction, and are thus adversarial against themselves, yet persist through dissensus.
