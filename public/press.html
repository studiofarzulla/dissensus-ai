01 · Boilerplate

How to describe us

Either description below can be quoted verbatim, in whole or in part. They are the canonical answers to “what is Dissensus?”

One line

Dissensus is a UK research lab formalising friction dynamics in multi-agent systems — the cost of coordination across markets, governance, cognition, and AI.

One paragraph

Dissensus is an independent UK research lab investigating adversarial systems — settings where competing interests generate structural conflict — through computational, empirical, and theoretical work. Its research programme, the Adversarial Systems & Complexity Research Initiative (ASCRI), treats friction as the through-line from markets and institutions to cognition and AI: coordination has overhead, and that overhead has structure. The lab is the research brand of Dissensus Ltd, registered in England and Wales (company no. 17309927), and publishes its research open access.
