Translation work with the Sineidocracia Movement

Dissensus is collaborating with Felipe Pachano Azuaje's Sineidocracia Movement on translation and open publication of the movement's work.

Dissensus is working with the Sineidocracia Movement, founded by Felipe Pachano Azuaje — readers of the About page will recognise him as the lab's cross-disciplinary affiliate — on the translation and open publication of the movement's work.

The collaboration covers English translation of the movement's texts, DOI assignment and archival deposit so the material becomes citable and permanent, and editorial support on forthcoming chapters. It is the kind of work the lab's open-science machinery exists for: the same pipeline that puts our papers on Zenodo with permanent identifiers works just as well for a body of political thought that deserves a wider readership than its current language confines it to.

Details of the first publications will follow here once they are settled.
