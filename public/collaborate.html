Research partnerships and collaboration proposals

We work with researchers who want to test, extend, or break the frameworks Dissensus builds. Co-authorship, critique, empirical testing, and institutional partnership are all on the table.

01 · What we're looking for

What we're looking for

We seek collaborators interested in rigorously testing the Axiom of Consent framework and related research. The objective is not consensus but productive dissensus, identifying failure modes and refining formalisations.

I

Co-Authorship

Joint papers on consent mechanics, machine morality, or economic microstructure. Empirical or theoretical.

II

Critical Review

Formal critiques of our frameworks. We publish strong counterarguments with full attribution.

III

Empirical Testing

Apply friction decomposition to new domains. Market data, political systems, agent simulations.

IV

Institutional Partnership

University affiliations, research grants, doctoral supervision. Scaling beyond independent work.
